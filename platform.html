The Platform

A platform that learns.

Every engagement, every estimate, every outcome feeds back into how you price and scope the next one. Servantium doesn't just store your data — it compounds your firm's intelligence over time.

What Servantium does

Four capabilities. One connected system.

Engagement Scoping & Pricing

  • Build estimates from your encoded service catalog
  • AI-generated estimates that improve from past engagement data
  • Tiered discount rules and custom pricing logic
  • Engagement templates for repeatable scoping

Document Generation

  • Generate proposals and SOWs from your estimates
  • Custom templates with full design control
  • PDF & DOCX output
  • Version history and audit trail

Resource & Delivery

  • Resource planning grid with capacity tracking
  • Project planning and team assignment
  • Pipeline analytics and dashboards
  • Engagement notes and client management

Intelligence That Compounds

  • Similar engagement search (semantic)
  • Institutional Memory Engine
  • Your estimates get sharper with every delivery
  • Knowledge doesn't walk out the door when people leave
