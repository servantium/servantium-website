## Create your account

<Steps>

1. **Navigate to [app.servantium.com](https://app.servantium.com)** and sign up with your work email.

2. **Set up your organization.** Enter your company name, industry, and team size. This helps Servantium tailor the experience.

3. **Invite your team.** Add colleagues who will be building engagements, creating quotes, or managing delivery.

</Steps>

## Create your first engagement

<Steps>

1. Click **Create Engagement** in the top bar.

2. Enter the **client name** and **engagement title** (e.g., "Acme Corp — CRM Implementation").

3. Add basic details: expected timeline, engagement type, and any notes from your initial conversations.

4. Navigate to the **Quotes** tab to start building your pricing.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
Start with your most common engagement type. Once you've built one end-to-end, you'll have a template for future work.
</Aside>

## Build a quote

In the Quotes tab of your engagement:

<Steps>

1. Click **Add Group** to create a pricing section (e.g., "Discovery & Requirements").

2. Add **line items** within each group — resources (people), materials, or fixed fees.

3. Set **quantities, costs, and markup** for each item. Servantium calculates totals automatically.

4. Review the **estimate total bar** at the bottom for a summary of cost, markup, and total.

</Steps>

## Generate a proposal

Once your quote is complete:

<Steps>

1. Navigate to the **Documents** tab.

2. Click **Generate SOW** to create a Statement of Work from your scoped engagement.

3. Review the generated document — it pulls scope, pricing, and deliverables directly from your quote.

4. Export as PDF or share a link with your client.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
Proposals are living documents. When you update your quote, regenerating the SOW reflects the latest scope and pricing automatically.
</Aside>
