## Create your account

<Steps>

1. **Navigate to [app.servantium.com](https://app.servantium.com)** and sign up with your work email.

2. **Set up your organization.** Enter your company name, industry, and team size. This helps Servantium tailor the experience.

3. **Invite your team.** Add colleagues who will be building engagements, creating quotes, or managing delivery.

</Steps>

## Create your first engagement

<Steps>

1. Click **Create Engagement** in the top bar (or the **+** button on mobile).

2. If your organization has engagement templates configured, select a type. Otherwise, a blank engagement is created.

3. Update the **engagement name** on the Info tab (e.g., "Acme Corp -- CRM Implementation").

4. Link an **account** (client company) and **contact**. You can create both inline if they don't exist yet.

5. Add **notes** from your initial conversations. These notes power AI features later -- the more context, the better.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
Start with your most common engagement type. Once you have built one end-to-end, you will have a pattern for future work.
</Aside>

## Build a quote

Navigate to the **Quotes** tab within your engagement:

<Steps>

1. Click **Create** to add a new quote and give it a title.

2. Click **Add Section** to create a pricing group (e.g., "Discovery & Requirements").

3. Add **line items** within each section. Start typing the item name -- Servantium suggests items from your Service Catalog with default costs and markups.

4. Set **quantities, costs, and markup** for each item. Totals calculate automatically.

5. Review the **estimate total bar** at the bottom for a summary of cost, markup, and total.

6. Or skip manual entry and click the **AI generate** button (sparkle icon) to have AI build the quote from your notes and similar past engagements.

</Steps>

## Generate a proposal

Once your quote is complete:

<Steps>

1. With your quote selected, click the **Generate Document** button (pen icon).

2. Select a **document template** from the dialog.

3. Servantium merges your engagement data, quote details, and organization information into a professional PDF.

4. The generated document appears in the engagement's **Documents** tab. Click to view it in the built-in PDF viewer.

</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
Documents are snapshots. When you update your quote, generate a new document to reflect the latest scope and pricing.
</Aside>
