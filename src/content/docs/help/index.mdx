## What is Servantium?

Servantium is a **CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote) and engagement management platform** built specifically for professional services businesses. It helps consulting firms, agencies, and service providers scope engagements, create accurate pricing, generate proposals, and manage delivery — all in one connected system.

Unlike generic CRM or PSA tools, Servantium captures the **complete engagement lifecycle** from initial discovery through delivery and learning, ensuring that knowledge compounds across your organization.
