## The Engagement

The **engagement** is the core unit of work in Servantium. It represents every client project from the first conversation through delivery and close-out.

An engagement captures:
- **Client and stakeholder information**
- **Scope and deliverables** (via quotes)
- **Resource assignments and capacity**
- **Notes and context** from every interaction
- **Documents** generated from your scoped work
- **Learning data** — what actually happened vs. what you estimated

<Aside type="tip">
Think of an engagement as a living record that gets richer over time. It starts as a lead and evolves into your organization's memory of that project.
</Aside>

## The Engagement Lifecycle

Every engagement flows through six phases:

| Phase | What happens |
|-------|-------------|
| **Discovery** | Understand client needs, capture context |
| **Estimation** | Build structured scope and pricing |
| **Proposal** | Generate deliverables from your estimate |
| **Activation** | Win the deal, assign the team |
| **Delivery** | Execute with real-time visibility |
| **Learning** | Capture outcomes, feed back into future estimates |

The key insight: **learning feeds back into discovery**. Each completed engagement makes your next estimate more accurate.

## Quotes

A **quote** is a structured pricing breakdown within an engagement. Quotes are built from:

- **Groups** — Sections like "Discovery & Requirements" or "Implementation"
- **Line items** — Resources (people), materials, or fixed fees within each group
- **Markup** — Applied per item or per group to reach your target margin

Quotes calculate **cost, markup percentage, and total estimate** automatically.

## Resources

**Resources** are your team members. Each resource has:
- A **role** (Lead Consultant, Solution Architect, Developer, etc.)
- A **cost rate** (your internal cost)
- **Availability** across time periods (month, week, day)

Resource planning shows you who's allocated where, highlights overallocation, and helps you staff engagements before they start.

## Documents

Servantium generates **Statements of Work (SOWs)** and other documents directly from your engagement data. Scope, deliverables, timeline, and pricing flow from the quote — no copy-pasting between tools.

## Notes & AI

**Notes** capture context from every client interaction. Servantium uses AI to:
- Extract key entities (people, technologies, timelines, budgets)
- Surface patterns across engagements
- Improve estimate accuracy over time

<Aside type="caution">
AI in Servantium assists — it doesn't decide. Human judgment stays in control. Every AI suggestion can be reviewed and overridden.
</Aside>
