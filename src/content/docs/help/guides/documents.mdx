## Generating a document

Servantium creates documents from your engagement's structured data — scope, pricing, deliverables, and timeline all flow from the quote.

<Steps>

1. Navigate to the **Documents** tab within your engagement.
2. Click **Generate SOW** (or choose another document type).
3. Review the generated preview — it includes project overview, deliverables, timeline, and total investment.
4. Edit any section directly in the preview.
5. Export as **PDF** or share a link.

</Steps>

## What's in a generated SOW?

| Section | Content source |
|---------|---------------|
| **Project Overview** | Engagement description and client details |
| **Deliverables & Timeline** | Quote groups mapped to phases with durations |
| **Investment Summary** | Quote totals broken down by phase |
| **Team & Resources** | Assigned resources from the resource plan |
| **Terms & Assumptions** | Configurable per engagement type |

<Aside type="tip">
Documents are **living**. When you update the quote or resource plan, regenerate the SOW to reflect the latest scope. No manual copy-paste required.
</Aside>

## Document templates

Customize the structure and language of your generated documents with templates. Control:

- Section order and headings
- Standard terms and conditions
- Branding and formatting
- Which data fields appear in each section

## Export options

- **PDF** — Polished, client-ready document
- **Shareable link** — View-only link for client review
- **DOCX** — Editable Word document (coming soon)
