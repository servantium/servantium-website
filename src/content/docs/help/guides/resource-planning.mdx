## Overview

Resource Planning gives you visibility into **who is working where, when, and for how long**. It surfaces overallocation before it becomes a problem and helps you staff new engagements based on actual availability.

## Views

### Month View

The default view shows resource allocation by month across all active engagements. Each cell shows planned hours with color coding:

| Color | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Green** | Normal utilization (within capacity) |
| **Amber** | Approaching capacity (80%+) |
| **Red** | Overallocated (exceeds available hours) |

### Week View

Drill into a specific week to see day-by-day allocation. Useful for sprint planning and short-term staffing decisions.

### Day View

Granular daily view for managing immediate workload.

## Assigning resources

Resources are assigned from the **Resource Plans** tab within an engagement. Assignments link directly to the roles and hours in your quote:

- The **Lead Consultant** from your quote's Discovery group becomes a resource assignment
- Hours planned in the quote flow into the resource plan
- Changes to the quote automatically update resource needs

<Aside type="note">
Resource assignments are distinct from quote line items. A single quote line item ("Solution Architect — 120 hours") may be split across multiple people or time periods in the resource plan.
</Aside>

## Capacity management

Servantium tracks each resource's **total available hours** per period and compares against their planned assignments across all engagements. This gives you:

- **Utilization rate** — Hours assigned vs. hours available
- **Overallocation alerts** — When someone is booked beyond capacity
- **Bench visibility** — Who has unallocated time for new work
