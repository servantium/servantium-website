## Engagement notes

Notes capture the context from every client interaction — discovery calls, requirements sessions, stakeholder meetings, technical discussions. Each note is attached to an engagement and timestamped.

### What makes notes powerful

Unlike meeting notes in a shared drive, Servantium notes are **structured and connected**:

- Attached to a specific engagement (not floating in a folder)
- AI-analyzed for key entities (people, technologies, timelines, budgets)
- Searchable across your organization
- Used to match similar engagements and improve future estimates

## AI capabilities

Servantium uses AI to augment your team's expertise — not replace it.

### Entity extraction

As you write notes, AI identifies and highlights:
- **People and roles** — Decision makers, stakeholders, technical contacts
- **Technologies** — Systems, platforms, and tools mentioned
- **Timelines and dates** — Deadlines, milestones, go-live targets
- **Budget signals** — Budget ranges, approval processes, fiscal year constraints

### Engagement intelligence

After delivery, AI analyzes the complete engagement record:
- **Estimate accuracy** — How close was your quote to actual cost?
- **Timeline variance** — Did delivery match planned duration?
- **Pattern detection** — What factors predict project success or risk?

<Aside type="caution">
AI insights are suggestions, not decisions. Every AI-generated recommendation includes the data it's based on so you can evaluate its reasoning. Human judgment stays in control.
</Aside>

### Semantic search

Search across all engagement notes using natural language. Find relevant past work by describing what you're looking for, not by remembering exact keywords.
