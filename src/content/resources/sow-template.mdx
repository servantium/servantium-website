The SOW is the most-edited document in every services firm. This template is the version we wish we'd had when we started.

**What's inside**
- Project overview and objectives
- Scope in, scope out, and assumptions
- Deliverables table with acceptance criteria
- Team, rates, and effort estimates
- Change control clause (the one clients will actually sign)
- Payment schedule and invoicing cadence
- Sign-off block
