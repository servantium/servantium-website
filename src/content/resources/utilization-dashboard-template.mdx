Before you buy a Professional Services OS, you need to know where you stand.

This template gives you the four numbers every services leader should check weekly: billable utilization, realization rate, WIP aging, and backlog coverage. Plug in your timesheet export and the sheet does the math.

**You'll learn**
- Which consultants are over- or under-utilized
- How much revenue you're leaving on the table (realization gap)
- How many weeks of runway your current backlog represents
- Where scope creep is quietly eroding margin
