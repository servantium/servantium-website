## Who each is for

**Kantata** is a mature resource planning and PSA tool, strong for mid-to-large consulting firms who need utilization reporting and capacity views and already have a mature ops function.

**Servantium** is the Professional Services OS for modern services firms who want scoping, pricing, delivery, and learning in one system — with AI drafting the first pass at every stage so the team compounds instead of re-inventing.

## Where Kantata is better (today)

- Deeper resource planning features for 200+ person firms
- More mature third-party integrations with legacy ERPs
- Longer customer track record and analyst coverage

## Where Servantium is better

- AI scoping and pricing built in, not bolted on
- One system across the full engagement lifecycle, not a PSA + 5 other tools
- Modern UX designed for partners, delivery leads, and consultants who actually use it daily
- Institutional memory: every engagement trains the next one

## The honest bottom line

If you're a 200+ person firm with an entrenched PSA process and a dedicated PMO, Kantata is the safe buy. If you're a 10–100 person modern services firm that wants one system to run the whole engagement lifecycle, Servantium was built for you.
