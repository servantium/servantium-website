Every services firm starts on spreadsheets. Most stay there longer than they should. Here's the honest trade-off.

## What spreadsheets do well
- Free, flexible, everyone knows how to use them
- Fast to set up, no procurement cycle
- No vendor lock-in

## Where spreadsheets break
- Version control chaos across the team
- No single source of truth for scope, rates, or margin
- Zero institutional memory — every engagement starts from scratch
- No audit trail when things go sideways
- You can't ask a spreadsheet "what did we quote on projects like this last year?"

## What Servantium gives you back
- One system of record for every engagement
- AI that learned from your last 50 projects
- Defensible pricing with historical anchoring
- Delivery discipline with real-time margin visibility
- Institutional memory that compounds

## The bottom line

If you're under 5 people and doing fewer than 10 engagements a year, stay on spreadsheets. If you're past that, every month you wait costs you real money.
